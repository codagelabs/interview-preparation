# ConnectWise -- Overview & Customers

## About ConnectWise

ConnectWise is a leading IT management and automation platform designed
primarily for Managed Service Providers (MSPs) and IT service
organizations.

Founded in 1982 and headquartered in Tampa, Florida (USA), ConnectWise
provides integrated tools that help IT teams manage service delivery,
automate operations, monitor systems, and scale their business.

ConnectWise offers a comprehensive platform that combines business management, remote monitoring, automation, cybersecurity, and support tools under one umbrella. These solutions are used by MSPs and internal IT teams to streamline operations and improve service delivery.
